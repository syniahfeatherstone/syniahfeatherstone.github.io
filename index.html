Mission Statment

Divergent Girls of Color is a new way to connect girls all over the world. We want to empower young women to be themselves and know they have the power to change the world. By connecting social media and social change we hope to redefine the perception of women. We want the site to be as interactive as possible and our users to feel free to embrace their true selves and have fun. Here we have fashion, hair and makeup hacks, news updates, beauty tips, countless opportunities and resources. We are unique, beautiful, divergent, intelligent, strong and powerful.
