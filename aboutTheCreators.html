About The Creator

My name is Syniah Featherstone. Divergent Girls Of Color means so much to me. I see far past my final project in Girls Who Code. I am grateful for the knowledge I have aquired and the help my teachers have given me. I am excited about the year I am about to embark on and look forward to taking these friends and skills with me. I never considered computer science before this past summer and I am so glad I did. It has opened a whole new world for me. I learned I have the power to create and build. I want DGC to be a place girls can come to as a resource to connect with others. I want young women of color to be empowered and know they can change the world. I have a vision for merchandise to be sold, updates on fashion, ethnic news, makeup and hair hacks and much more. I want our users to feel safe in an enviroment made just for them. Away from the sterotypes and negativity DGC is a safe place. My contact info: syniahfeatherstone@gmail.com
