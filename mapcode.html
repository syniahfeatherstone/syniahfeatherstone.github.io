NORDSTROM

Nordstrom carries Laura Mercier and Nars. Laura Mercier has great products like their tinted moisturizer, secret concealer, and deluxe finishing powder. NARS cosmetics is a well known brand but the brand's foundations are appealing to women with darker skin tones because they offer both soft satin and matte finishes; meanwhile, the cult Creamy Concealer ($29) comes in a deep cocoa color that's perfect for brightening up under-eyes on darker skin tones.

ULTA BEAUTY

Ulta is also a great place to go it carries Bobbi Brown. Makeup artist Bobbi Brown is known for advocating for shades that look natural on a wide range of complexions and her namesake line never disappoints, specifically Bobbi Brown Skin Foundation SPF 15, $48, and Bobbi Brown Long-Wear Even Finish Compact Foundation, $46. Both products come in 20 shades, half of which range from tan to espresso and offer six variations of the darkest skin tones.

Beauty Tips

There are several ways to determine your foundation, lip, and eyeshadow color. Based off of undertones, nationality, and even eye color. This can help not only with your makeup selection but also clothing color choices and accessories. Here are a few quick pieces of advice.

FOR BLUE-EYED GIRLS

Try some simple lighter earthy tones at first then if your feeling bold look up some daring looks of your favorite youtubers using smokey browns, green, and even a dash of vibrant colors to bring out the best you. Light, neutral tones best complemeheadnt cool blue eyes. Steer clear of anything harsh, like an overly dark, smokey look. FOR GREEN-EYED GIRLS

Your eyes are naturally bright, so you don't have to rely on vivid shadows and liners to attract attention. A muted purple is the perfect pop for a night out, and a shimmery brown is just enough color during the day. You also look great in coffee type browns and deeper colors for your set of eyes that contrast in color from light to dark to really bring out the inner you. A tiny tip that you may also know is mascara. Playing in different colored mascara can really add to a look in the simplest way. Here are a few cool colors to try

FOR DEEP BROWN-EYED GIRLS

Yaaaaaaas the eye color most of us colored girls fall into, so it must be a special color. Our eyes may not always stand out in a crowd, but we are definitely special in our own individual ways. Dusting lids with rich pigment amps up dark eyes. Create the illusion of larger eyes with strong shades like violet, silver and chocolate. You can accentuate dark eyes even more with black liner in the waterline to define. Any really deep shadows will actually bring out the eyes despite most thoughts. The deep shadows will give your eyes that mystery we all like. Also never forget black eyeliner is ALWAYS your friend, define those eyes! Here are a few cool colors to try

FOR MEDIUM/LIGHT BROWN and HAZEL EYED GIRLS

Not only do you vary in awesomness but you possess gold literally in your eyes. For the brown eyed girls (almost) anything goes. Most colors work well with beautiful brown eyes, but neutral shades like salmon and bronzy gold really accentuate them. Whatever color you choose, try blending the crease with black shadow to add a touch of intensity and smokey eye. For the hazel eyed girls choose the metallics and pastels in your palette. Heavy, smoky shadows tend to hide the subtle hints of green and gold in multitonal eyes. On the other hand, delicate metallics and dusty pinks intensify the flecks and make light brown eyes sparkle.
